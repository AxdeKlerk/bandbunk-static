Both Ways.

Artists prove they're real — by linking their social profiles and supplying the venue they're playing. Hosts know exactly who's knocking on their door.

After every stay, both sides rate the experience. Leave the place a tip? Zero stars and a public comment. Hosts that are rude or sketchy? Same deal. The community polices itself.
