The Basics

By using BandBunk you agree to: be honest in your listings and booking requests, only use the platform for genuine music-related stays, treat hosts and artists with respect, and leave honest reviews.

Hosts

Listings must accurately represent the space offered. Minimum price is £10/night. Breakfast (tea/coffee and toast at minimum) must be included. Confirmed bookings are a commitment — cancelling without good reason harms the community.

Artists

You must be a genuine band or artist playing a real gig. Providing false venue or event information is grounds for immediate account suspension. Respect the host's space and rules.

Reviews

Reviews must be honest and based on real experiences. Fake or malicious reviews will be removed and may result in account suspension.

Payments

All payments are processed securely on confirmation of booking. BandBunk takes no responsibility for disputes arising from off-platform payment arrangements.
